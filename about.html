Story

I grew up watching people before I ever learned to market to them.

Mumbai kid, moved around a lot, picked up pieces from every place and every person I met. IT, analytics, brand strategy. None of it was a straight line, but all of it added up.

I just graduated from Virginia Tech and honestly, the thing I love most is that messy space where data meets a real human conversation and something actually clicks. I'm the person who gets way too excited pulling apart why people do what they do.

Yes I can do the technical stuff. Python, NLP, conjoint analysis, demand forecasting. But what I actually care about is making all that complexity disappear. Taking a wall of data and turning it into something a team looks at and goes "oh, now I get it."

Right now I'm somewhere in the overlap between analytics, AI, and behavioral research. Trying to figure out how to make marketing that's smart without losing the human part. That's the whole thing for me.
