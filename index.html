Story

From people-watching to practical strategy.

I grew up in Mumbai and moved across disciplines. From IT to digital marketing, carrying pieces of every detour with me. That mix of cultures, timelines, and people is how I understand audiences today.

I'm a digital marketing strategist who blends quantitative rigor with creative intuition. A recent Virginia Tech graduate with a degree in Digital Marketing Strategy (3.97 GPA), I learn as much from customer behavior models and sentiment analysis as I do from real conversations. I take messy inputs—briefs, datasets, market signals—and turn them into campaigns that are both analytically sound and emotionally resonant.

My work sits at the intersection of data science and storytelling. I build segmentation models, run conjoint analyses, and forecast demand—then translate those insights into strategic recommendations that teams can actually act on. Whether it's Python for NLP, Enginius for decision modeling, or slide decks for stakeholder presentations, I focus on making complexity clear.

Right now I'm exploring how advanced analytics, AI tools, and behavioral research can work together to create marketing that's both intelligent and human-centered.
