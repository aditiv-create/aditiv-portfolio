About
Who I am beyond bullet points
Strategy brain, design brain, and “let’s actually ship this” brain in one person.

I grew up in Mumbai and moved across disciplines. From IT to digital marketing, carrying pieces of every detour with me. That mix of cultures, timelines, and people is how I understand audiences today.

Story

From people-watching to practical strategy.

I’m a digital marketing strategist who learns as much from overheard conversations and random podcasts as I do from dashboards. I like taking messy inputs; briefs, data, & real human behaviour and turning them into campaigns that feel clear, considered, and human.

A lot of my work sits where empathy, structure, and aesthetics overlap. I care about how something feels to scroll through, but also about why it exists, who it serves, and whether it respects people’s attention.

Right now I’m especially interested in how AI, analytics, and storytelling can work together to simplify decisions instead of overwhelming people.
