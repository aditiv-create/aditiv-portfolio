11 — Reaching Parents

Where parents
actually are.

The campaign targets parents of UK 15–17 year olds. The media plan follows where they actually are.

Facebook / Meta

93% reach among UK adults. Average 43 minutes daily. The dominant platform for 35–55 year olds and the primary channel for parent-to-parent content. #FirstCardMoment campaign seeded here first.

Mumsnet

8 million monthly active UK users. 89% female. 35–54 age group = 39% of all users (~3.1 million people). 96% trust product recommendations. 1 in 2 UK households with children under 19. The highest-trust parenting channel in the UK.

Instagram / Threads

63% reach among UK women. Reels now account for 35–50% of time on platform. Threads amplifies parent community conversation. Parent Reels targeting: interest-overlaid against 'family travel,' 'teen finance,' 'parenting 15–17 year olds.'

Travel placement

Contextual advertising on Skyscanner, Booking.com, and TUI when parents search European destinations — the moment of maximum receptivity. A parent planning a trip for their teenager is exactly where this campaign belongs.
